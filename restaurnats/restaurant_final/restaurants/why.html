Our Journey

How It All Started

2021

The Idea

After one too many disappointing restaurant choices, our founder decided to build a smarter way to discover where to eat.

2022

First 50 Restaurants

We hand-picked and reviewed the first 50 restaurants in Chiang Mai, establishing our quality benchmark.

2023

Expanding to Bangkok & Phuket

Growing demand pushed us to expand our coverage across Thailand's major culinary hubs.

2024

Member Card System Launch

We introduced member cards to reward loyal diners with exclusive discounts and early access.

Now

200+ Restaurants & Growing

DarlinG continues to grow, driven by our community of food lovers and our passion for great dining.
